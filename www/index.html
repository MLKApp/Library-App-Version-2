Circulation Desk

The Circulation Desk staff provide assistance with materials checkout, course reserves, library accounts, Link+ and ILL holds, and paying fines.

Printing, Scanning, and Copying

You can print from any library computer, USB, or laptop. Printing stations can be found on the lower level, 1st floor, 2nd floor, and 5th floor.

  • Printing - Lower Level
  • Printing - Mezzanine
  • Printing - 1st Floor
  • Printing - 2nd Floor
  • Printing - 5th Floor

Mezzanine

The Mezzanine is an SJSU student-only space for collaborating, studying, or just relaxing between classes. It is located between the lower level and 1st floor.

  • Mezzanine

Student Computing Services - Laptops and iPads

Student Computing Services provides students free access to PC laptops, MacBooks and iPads. They are located on the 4th floor. You can also check out iPads and laptops on the Mezzanine.

  • Find the check out station on the Mezzanine.
  • Find Student Computing Services on the 4th Floor.
  • More about Student Computing Services on the SJSU Library website.

Library Cards

A library card and PIN are needed to borrow items or get access to ebooks and databases. Bring your Tower Card to the Circulation Desk to get a library card.

  • More information on the SJSU Library Website
  • Get help at the Circulation Desk on the 1st Floor.

  • Find your subject librarian on the SJSU Library website.
